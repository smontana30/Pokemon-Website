Pokemon Gym Leaders

There are a total of sixteen gym leaders in both Kanto and Johto. Eight in Kanto and the other eight in Johto. The trainer must battle and defeat all eight trainers in order to their gym badges. Each gym leader will specialize in one specific Pokemon type and will also have their gym designed to fit the environment of that specific type. If a trainer is able to win eight gym badges then they are eligible to compete agains the region's Elite Four. Since each gym leader specialize in a specific pokemon type you will have to be careful and pick your pokemon wisely for our battle.
